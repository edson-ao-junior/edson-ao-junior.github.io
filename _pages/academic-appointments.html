In addition to my research and teaching activities, I have held several academic leadership and service roles at the State University of Maringá (UEM), as well as national-level academic service appointments in Brazil. These roles reflect my sustained involvement in undergraduate and graduate program management, curriculum development, institutional governance, the coordination of open science initiatives within the graduate program, and the evaluation of research and graduate education policies at institutional, state, and national levels.

Professional and Multidisciplinary Program Leadership

Multidisciplinary Coordinator
Technical Residency Program in Forensic Sciences
State University of Maringá (UEM), Brazil
In partnership with: Secretariat of Science, Technology and Higher Education (SETI) and the Paraná Scientific Police
Period: 2022 – 2024

As Multidisciplinary Coordinator, I was responsible for the academic and operational coordination of the Technical Residency Program in Forensic Sciences, a strategic initiative established through a formal agreement between UEM, SETI, and the Paraná Scientific Police.
